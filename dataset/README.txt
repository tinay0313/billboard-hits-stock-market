Raw Data:
The Billboard Hot 100 and Song Attributes data are downloaded from https://www.kaggle.com/danield2255/data-on-songs-from-billboard-19992019.
Files used are "billboardHot100_1999-2019.csv" and "artistDf.csv" (renamed to "songAttributes_1999-2019.csv").

The S&P 500 data is scraped from Yahoo Finance (sp500_1999_2020.csv).

Processed Data:
"billboardHot100_1999-2019.csv" is cleaned and preprocessed using "dataClean.py" to output "billboard_input1.csv".
"sp500_1999_2000.csv" is rendered through "yLabel.py" to output "yLabel.csv".
